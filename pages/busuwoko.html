1-Day Kampala City Tour

A vibrant snapshot of Uganda’s capital culture, history, and breathtaking views all in one day.

Main Destinations: Uganda Museum; Kabaka’s Palace & Idi Amin’s chambers; Kasubi Royal Tombs; Gaddafi Mosque; Buganda Road Craft Market, Independence Monument,

    Itinerary

  • Guided visit to the Uganda Museum

    • Explore Kabaka’s Palace & Idi Amin’s torture chambers

  • Tour of Kasubi Royal Tombs (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Visit the Gaddafi Mosque for panoramic views
  • Shopping at Buganda Road Craft Market

    • Inclusions

  • Professional guide
  • Entry fees to attractions
  • Transport in an air-conditioned vehicle
  • Lunch and bottled drinking water

    • Exclusions

  • Tips & personal expenses
  • Alcoholic drinks
  • Extra activities not listed

    • Day 2

  • Morning game drive where you get to see a variety of wild animals like lions, giraffes, several species of antelopes, elephants and birds.
  • After the game drive in the afternoon, hop into a boat cruise where you will get to see hippos, Nile crocodiles and the bottom of the falls.

    • Day 3

  • Hike to the top of the falls on the last day.
  • Heading back to kampala.

    • Pricing: Budget — $750 per person | Luxury — $1,700 per person
