Busowoko falls

located in Jinja, is known for it's stunning natural beauty, particularly it's picturesque waterfall and the tranquil atmosphere surrounding them. It's a popular destination for kayaking and rafting on the Nile river as well as nature walks and bird watching. The falls also offer scenic view points, perfect photography and relaxation.
