3-Day Murchison Falls Safari

Discover Africa’s most powerful waterfall and Uganda’s largest savanna park. Main Destinations: Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary; Murchison Falls National Park; Nile River.

    Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Rhino trekking at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary, drive to Murchison, evening visit to Top of the Falls.
  • Day 2: Morning game drive for lions, giraffes, elephants, and buffalo
  • Afternoon Nile boat cruise; optional hike to the top of the falls.
  • Day 3: Optional sunrise game drive; return journey to Kampala/Entebbe.

    • Inclusions:

  • Park entrance fees
  • Rhino permit and tracking
  • Game drives & boat cruise
  • Accommodation (mid-range or luxury)
  • All meals & bottled water
  • Professional driver/guide
  • Transport in a 4x4 safari vehicle or a Van

    • Exclusions:

  • Tips & gratuities
  • Alcoholic drinks
  • Optional activities not listed
    • Pricing:Budget — $750 per person | Luxury — $2,750 per person
    Note: We do airport pick up for free upon request
