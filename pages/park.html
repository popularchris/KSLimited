5-Day Gorillas & Queen Elizabeth Safari

A bucket-list journey — trek with gentle giants and explore Uganda’s most iconic savanna

Main Destinations: Bwindi Impenetrable Forest; Queen Elizabeth National Park; Ishasha Secto

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kampala to Bwindi, Equator stop, scenic countryside views
  • Day 2: Gorilla trekking in Bwindi; optional cultural community visit
  • Day 3: Transfer to Queen Elizabeth via Ishasha, chance to see tree-climbing lions
  • Day 4: Morning game drive; Kazinga Channel boat cruise
  • Day 5: Return journey to Kampala/Entebbe
