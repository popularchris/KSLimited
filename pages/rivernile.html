1-Day Jinja Source of the Nile Adventure (Ranging $95-$150)

At the heart of Uganda’s adventure capital, culture, and legend meet at the source of the Nile. Here, sacred waterfalls whisper old tales, lush forests, and the world’s longest river begins its timeless journey. To stand in Jinja is to feel history. Main Destinations: Sezibwa Falls; Mabira Forest; Jinja Town; Source of the Nile.

    Itinerary;

  • Pick up from your hotel by our staff
  • Scenic drive throughout Kampala to Jinja with cultural and nature stops.
  • Stop at Sezibwa Falls for a guided walk and cultural legends.
  • Guided nature walk in Mabira Forest, expect to see monkeys, butterflies, and exotic flora.
  • Lunch by the Nile River.
  • Source of the Nile: Boat ride to the exact starting point of the Nile, bird watching.
  • Explore Jinja town and craft shops, and interact with the locals
  • Return to Kampala by 6 pm
  • Drop off at your hotel location
