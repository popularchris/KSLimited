12-Day Grand Uganda Safari

The ultimate Ugandan adventure — from rhinos to gorillas, waterfalls to crater lakes.

Main Destinations: Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary; Murchison Falls; Kibale; Queen Elizabeth; Bwindi; Lake Bunyonyi; Lake Mburo

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival and transfer to the hotel
  • Day 2-3: Murchison Falls NP — game drives, Nile cruise, Top of the Falls
  • Day 4-5: Kibale Forest — chimp trekking, Bigodi swamp walk
  • Day 6-7: Queen Elizabeth NP — game drives, Kazinga Channel cruise
  • Day 8-9: Bwindi — gorilla trekking, optional cultural experiences
  • Day 10: Relaxation at Lake Bunyonyi
  • Day 11: Lake Mburo NP — game drive, optional cycling/walking safari
  • Day 12: Return to Entebbe for departure
