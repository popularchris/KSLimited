About US

Where Travel Meets Connection!


Welcome to Kenma Tours & Safaris, your doorway into Uganda, the Pearl of Africa. We design immersive, personalized travel experiences that go beyond sightseeing. Whether you’re visiting for a romantic escape, research, a long-awaited return, or pure adventure, we help you connect deeply with the story of the pearl of Africa and it's people. At Kenma, travel is more than movement; it’s meaning. It’s feeling the pulse of Africa, walking alongside its communities, and rediscovering your sense of belonging. Our tours are crafted for solo travelers, couples, families, and global citizens seeking real, rooted, and enriching journeys. Come as a guest. Leave as family and carry Uganda with you always.

Our Vision

Transforming travel into a journey of connection and discovery in uganda’s breathtaking landscapes and vibrant cultures. We aspire to to make every safari an experience that renews,deepen connections and creates lasting bonds between travelers and the Pearl of Africa.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable travel experiences that reveal Uganda's breathtaking beauty, rich culture, inspiring connection, belonging, and discovery for every traveler.
